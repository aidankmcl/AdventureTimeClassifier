<p>"<b>Hug Wolf</b>" is the eighth episode in the fourth season of <i><a href="/wiki/Adventure_Time" title="Adventure Time">Adventure Time</a></i>. It is the eighty-sixth episode overall.
</p>
<nav id="toc" class="toc"><div id="toctitle"><h2>Contents</h2><span class="toctoggle">[<a href="#" class="internal" id="togglelink" data-show="show" data-hide="hide">show</a>]</span></div><ol></ol></nav>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span></h2>
<p>After an encounter with a <a href="/wiki/Beautiful_Lady" title="Beautiful Lady">Hug Wolf</a>, <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> goes through a Jekyll and Hyde-like transformation, becoming a <a href="/wiki/Hug_Wolves" title="Hug Wolves">hug wolf</a> himself.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span></h2>
<p>The episode begins with Finn and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> exploring the woods and trying to find and slay the <a href="/wiki/Tree_of_Blight" title="Tree of Blight">Tree of Blight</a>. When they find the tree, a Hug Wolf jumps out of the forest and attempts to give Finn a hug. As Jake goes to kill the Tree of Blight, the Hug Wolf latches on to Finn and roughly hugs him. Jake sets fire to the tree as flaming seedpods burst from the tree and hits the Hug Wolf on the snout causing it to flee. The two protagonists return home.
</p><p>Later, at their <a href="/wiki/Tree_Fort" title="Tree Fort">tree house</a>, Finn starts to act strange by hugging <a href="/wiki/BMO" title="BMO">BMO</a>. Jake asks Finn if he is alright and checks his temperature with his paw noting that Finn has a fever. Finn then hugs BMO harder causing BMO to state that he was hurting it. Jake tells the feverish Finn to go to bed as it could help. He goes to bed and starts moving around, hugging his pillow, and acting uncomfortable. The camera pans to show the window which shows the full moon, and a howl is heard.
</p><p>The scene shifts to the next day, Finn (who is feeling better) and Jake are picking up candy litter in the <a href="/wiki/Candy_Kingdom" title="Candy Kingdom">Candy Kingdom</a>, which they plan to eat for lunch, when suddenly, <a href="/wiki/Cinnamon_Bun" title="Cinnamon Bun">Cinnamon Bun</a> bumps into Finn and begs him to not hug him again due to the discomfort caused to his "love handles." Finn, confused, states, "I didn't hug you last night." Finn and CB laugh as Finn makes fun of "crazy old Cinnamon Bun." Cinnamon Bun leaves and Finn and Jake finish their candy.
</p><p>Jake starts to wonder if the hugging started because of the hug wolf that hugged Finn, so they go to a library and Jake gets a book out and reads a page explaining about the hug wolf. They discover that an alpha hug wolf can create new hug wolves by hugging someone under a full moon. They learn that every night a hug wolf must fulfill a crazy hug lust and there was no known cure for the transformation. Finn tries to hug Jake, but he stops him exclaiming "No Hugs!" Finn apologizes and comments how he felt hot and begins to hug himself.
</p><p>Later that night, Finn and Jake are in the Tree Fort. As Finn is sitting down, Jake places bags of frozen peas on Finn to cool him down. They notice that BMO is hiding behind a potted plant and quivering at the sight of Finn and stating "I am terrified of Finn." Jake tries to get Finn to overcome the urge of hugging by shouting, and later by imagining of hugging a cactus. Despite his efforts, Finn begins to lust for hugs and Jake is forced to chain up Finn to a chair. Jake then hides behind the plant with BMO as BMO states they need a "silver baseball." Finn then transforms into a hug wolf and breaks free from the chains then Jake told Finn to say something so that he can be sure but all Finn said was "I'll hug your <a href="/wiki/Margaret" title="Margaret">Mom</a>!" Finn walks up to Jake and BMO to hug them, but Jake tells BMO to use his "strobe light mode," which causes Finn to jump out of the window and run towards the Candy Kingdom. In the Candy Kingdom, Finn runs around and hugs <a href="/wiki/Candy_Person_3" title="Candy Person 3">Mr. Cupcake</a> and then breaks into a <a href="/wiki/Gumdrop_teenager" title="Gumdrop teenager">little gumdrop girl</a>'s room. He attempts to hug the girl when <a href="/wiki/Gumdrop_Dad" title="Gumdrop Dad">her dad</a> walks in with a <a href="/wiki/Candy_cane_shotgun" title="Candy cane shotgun">candy cane shotgun</a> and attempts to shoot Finn, but he gets away.
</p><p>The next morning, Finn wakes up in a stream in the Candy Kingdom with Jake over him. Finn asks what happened when Jake replies "You hugged everyone!" Various <a href="/wiki/Candy_People" title="Candy People">Candy People</a> are on the bridge over the stream and are yelling at Finn, with a small green ice cream man saying "We're mad, but we're not gonna go down there..." after the candy people stop yelling. Finn then apologizes to the Candy People and requests for them to lock him up in the <a href="/wiki/Candy_Dungeon" title="Candy Dungeon">Candy Dungeon</a>. They lock him in the dungeon while Jake and a few Candy People form a mob to go after the Alpha Hug Wolf. Cinnamon Bun is put in charge of keeping Finn locked up.
</p><p>After they leave, the moon comes out, and Finn changes into a Hug Wolf again. Finn asks Cinnamon Bun if he wants a hug, and he replies, "I can't, man." Finn then inquires, "Not even a little one?" Then Cinnamon Bun replies, "YES!" and starts opening the cage. After opening the door, Finn jumps out and gives Cinnamon Bun a huge hug, hurting him. Finn then runs away. It scene cuts to the mob finding the alpha hug wolf where the incident took place. Jake says, "I smell someone being hugged right now," and sees a deer being hugged by the wolf. Jake and the mob start yelling. The hug wolf then confronts the mob as Jake apologizes to Finn for failing him. Suddenly, Finn appears and jumps on the alpha wolf, and starts hugging it. The two hug each other until they both started to glow and bright light bursts out from their hugs. The aftermath reveals that the two hug wolves hugging was the cure as Finn is back to normal and the alpha hug wolf turned into a humanoid woman. Jake notices the beauty of the girl and asks "Can I get a hug?" then Finn angrily calls out Jake's rudeness, "JAKE!" The episode ends with the girl turning into a Tree of Blight causing Jake to scream in fear at her actual appearance.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span></h2>
<ul><li>When Jake pulls out a book to look up hug wolves, it is titled <a href="/wiki/Bestiarium_Vocabulum" title="Bestiarium Vocabulum">Bestiarium Vocabulum</a> which means "Compendium of Beasts."
</li><li>BMO has a strobe-light mode.
</li><li><a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a> makes a cameo, chasing a black <a href="/wiki/Cat_(Hug_Wolf)" title="Cat (Hug Wolf)" class="mw-redirect">cat</a>. It was likely a reference to Natasha's black cat Pancake.
</li><li>After Finn breaks the curse on both him and the Alpha Hug Wolf, the music from the title card plays.
</li><li>At the end of the episode when Jake is screaming after the <a href="/wiki/Beautiful_Lady" title="Beautiful Lady">Beautiful Lady</a> turns into another <a href="/wiki/Tree_of_Blight" title="Tree of Blight">Tree of Blight</a>, one can see that Jake is missing a tooth.
</li><li>Even though <a href="/wiki/Gumdrop_Dad" title="Gumdrop Dad">Gumdrop Dad</a> makes fake gunshots and shoot sounds with his mouth, the <a href="/wiki/Candy_cane_shotgun" title="Candy cane shotgun">candy cane</a> he uses can inflict real damage.
</li><li>When Finn is tied up in the tree fort, an object that looks a lot like <a href="/wiki/CMO" title="CMO">CMO</a> can be seen inside of one of the holes in the wall.
</li><li> Just before Jake slaps the Alpha Hug Wolf, after saying he can smell someone being hugged, one can see ".- -" carved into the tree he hides behind. .- - is Morse Code for AT, presumably meaning Adventure Time.
</li><li> There are several Morse Code messages in the library where Jake reads the Bestiarium Vocabulum.
<ul><li> In the trimming on the tablecloth: .-. --.. -....- ...- -  meaning "APRILAT"
</li><li> On the large, one-eyed skull on a shelf to the left: .... . .-- .-.. --- meaning "HEWLO"
</li><li> Near the bottom of the shelf: ---.. ..--- ---- ---- meaning "82??" (The input ---- has no meaning in Morse Code)
</li><li> Near the bottom of the shelf behind Finn (in the second scene): -... ----. ..--- meaning "B92" (This may not be correct, as the exact code is hard to make out.)
</li><li> On the drawer to the right of Finn (in the second scene): .--- meaning "J"
</li></ul>
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cultural_references">Cultural references</span></h3>
<ul><li>When Finn transforms in front of Jake and BMO, BMO tells Jake that they need a silver baseball. This is a reference to a silver bullet being the only thing that can kill a werewolf, and also referencing <i><a href="/wiki/Portender_Defender" title="Portender Defender">Portender Defender</a></i>.
</li><li>The table in the library where Jake found the book has a symbol somewhat like the Millennium Eye from the Japanese anime <i>Yu-Gi-Oh!</i>
</li><li>Mr. Cupcake refers to Finn as a lycanthrope when he is being attacked, another name for a werewolf.
</li><li>The scenes where Finn is hunting for people to hug in the first person and the final battle between Finn and the alpha werewolf are likely references to the movie <i>Wolfman</i> and the TV show <i>Teen Wolf</i>.
</li><li>Also Jake chaining Finn is a reference to Stiles chaining Scott in <i>Teen Wolf</i>.
</li></ul>

</li></ul>
<ul><li>In her cameo, Princess Bubblegum is wearing the same outfit that she wore at the end of "<a href="/wiki/Video_Makers" title="Video Makers">Video Makers</a>" and in "<a href="/wiki/The_Real_You" title="The Real You">The Real You</a>."
</li></ul>
