</script><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span></h2>
<p>The episode starts with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> wearing <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> as the "Jakesuit," smashing around the <a href="/wiki/Tree_Fort" title="Tree Fort">Tree Fort</a> and letting <a href="/wiki/BMO" title="BMO">BMO</a> attack him as hard as it can, not realizing that he is causing Jake pain. Finn then jumps off the roof and lands crotch first on a fence, causing Jake to spit out Finn. BMO comes outside and hits Jake one last time before the screen goes black and the scene goes to Finn playing BMO on the couch. Jake has a red lower body as he limps onto the couch to lie down. As Jake recovers, he chastises Finn while Finn tells him pain is easy to take. Jake challenges this by shrinking down, hopping into Finn's body and taking control of it to wear him like a suit until he admits that pain is not fun. Unfortunately, Jake is not as "graceful" as Finn and cannot control his body well enough to physically hurt him. Instead, he gets <a href="/wiki/Dream_Journal_of_a_Boring_Man,_Vol._12" title="Dream Journal of a Boring Man, Vol. 12">Dream Journal of a Boring Man, Vol. 12</a> to make Finn read it, hoping to torture him with boredom; however, the plan backfires as Jake falls asleep reading the book while Finn becomes enthralled with it. After Jake wakes up they get hungry and Finn offers a truce so Jake can make them lunch. Finn recommends that Jake use this as an opportunity to cause Finn pain. Jake makes a delicious meatloaf, Finn's favorite food. Jake eats it all himself to get Finn to admit he is wrong, but Finn is completely undeterred.
</p><p>Finn's <a href="/wiki/Special_occasion_phone" title="Special occasion phone">special occasion phone</a> suddenly rings and Finn notices that <a href="/wiki/Flame_Princess" title="Flame Princess">Flame Princess</a> is calling. Finn says that he is not going to answer because Jake might embarrass him, which gives Jake the idea to do so. Jake forces Finn to waddle over to <a href="/wiki/Flame_Princess%27s_house" title="Flame Princess's house">Flame Princess's house</a>, where she meets them outside. She says that she was worried because Finn didn't answer the phone, and that she has invited the "less evil" members of her family to meet Finn over dinner. Jake then forces Finn to take off his clothes, stick the dining cloth in his underwear while Jake sings <a href="/wiki/Baby_Finn_Song" title="Baby Finn Song" class="mw-redirect">Finn's baby song</a> in a high-pitched voice. Flame Princess and her family are aghast at this until Flame Princess sees that Jake is controlling Finn and learns of the bet. She wishes Finn the best of luck with the bet and they leave.
</p><p>They go to <a href="/wiki/Lady_Rainicorn%27s_house" title="Lady Rainicorn's house">Lady Rainicorn's house</a>, where Lady is seen washing dishes when she sees Finn. They go inside and she asks where Jake is. Jake then stretches out his head and says that he is in Finn's body, as <a href="/wiki/T.V." title="T.V.">T.V.</a> passes by and says hi to them while heading to his computer. When Jake tells Lady of the bet and asks for advice on how to win it, T.V. advises he jump into a volcano. Jake takes the advice, goes to a volcano and, by "fortuitous circumstance," meets some <a href="/wiki/Fire_Wolves" title="Fire Wolves">fire wolves</a>, whom Jake uses kibble to bait into attacking Finn. However, they just eat the kibble and lick Finn's face, and their heat does not bother Finn at all. Jake takes Finn to the volcano and starts to lower him into the lava; however, once it becomes clears that Finn is not afraid of getting his skull burned in lava, Jake says to himself that Finn is crazy and pulls him out of the volcano, leaves his body and admits defeat. Finn rejoices and jumps into Jake to form Jakesuit again, and jumps into the volcano just for fun.
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/Candy_Kingdom" title="Candy Kingdom">Candy Kingdom</a>'s hospital, Finn and Jake are recovering in full body casts when the <a href="/wiki/Clown_Nurse" title="Clown Nurse">Clown Nurses</a> show up, much to Finn's horror and Jake's delight.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span></h2>
<ul><li>This episode's title is similar to one of the scrapped episodes, "<a href="/wiki/Jakesuit" title="Jakesuit">Jakesuit</a>," which also involved Finn wearing Jake as a suit.
</li><li>It's revealed that T.V. still lives with his mom, and that he can speak English as of this episode.
</li><li>When Jake is singing the <a href="/wiki/Baby_Finn_Song" title="Baby Finn Song" class="mw-redirect">Baby Finn Song</a>, he sings the first line incorrectly, singing "I'm a tough tootin' baby" rather than "I'm a buff buff baby." The line Jake sings does not come into the song until later.
</li><li>About a minute into the episode, where Finn breaks the tree fort, Ice King can be seen.
</li><li>When Jake made Finn pull his own hat, his hair could briefly be seen.
</li><li> This episode shows that Finn has an almost non-existent gag reflex, given that he can take having an immobile object in his throat for a long period of time without coughing, gagging, or vomiting.
</li><li> In this episode, Finn is noticed to somehow have a slight occasional Sadomasochist behavior, someone who enjoys pain and loves inflicting pain, also Finn doesn't enjoy loosing especially to a bet or to Jake.
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cultural_references">Cultural references</span></h3>
<ul><li>The name of the Icelandic musician <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bj%C3%B6rk" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:Björk">Björk</a> is used as an exclamation in this episode three times: once by Finn, once by Flame Princess and once by Jake.
</li><li>While Finn swings around the Tree House in the Jake Suit, music plays similar to the music played in the Spider-Man movies while Spider-Man swings around on a web.
</li><li>The book Jake makes Finn read to torture him could be a reference to <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Diary_of_a_Nobody" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:The Diary of a Nobody">The Diary of a Nobody</a></i>, similar in title and premise.
</li><li>When the Clown Nurses arrive, one of them sings the "Entry of the Gladiators" by Julius Fucik, a motif generally used in association with the circus.
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Episode_connections">Episode connections</span></h3>
<ul><li>Finn last wore Jake as a suit in the episode "<a href="/wiki/The_Silent_King" title="The Silent King">The Silent King</a>." Finn comments on this, saying "Man, I haven't worn the Jake Suit in a while!"
</li><li>This is the first time Jake shows he knows the <a href="/wiki/Baby_Finn_Song" title="Baby Finn Song" class="mw-redirect">Baby Finn Song</a>; previously it was only seen within Finn's mind as in "<a href="/wiki/Memory_of_a_Memory" title="Memory of a Memory">Memory of a Memory</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/Goliad_(episode)" title="Goliad (episode)">Goliad</a>." However, it is unknown how he knew this.
</li><li>The <a href="/wiki/Clown_Nurse" title="Clown Nurse">Clown Nurses</a> from "<a href="/wiki/Another_Way" title="Another Way">Another Way</a>" appears at the end of the episode to help Finn and Jake recover from their burns. Finn's horror and Jake's delight with the nurses also appear.
</li><li>Jake brings up his romantic ranking system from "<a href="/wiki/Burning_Low" title="Burning Low">Burning Low</a>" again when he is about to send Finn into the volcano saying, "The Universe is going to have a full-on make-out session with you, bro. I'm talkin' minimum tier 8."
</li><li>As revealed in "<a href="/wiki/Puhoy" title="Puhoy">Puhoy</a>," <a href="/wiki/Rainicorns" title="Rainicorns">rainicorn</a> children age quickly, all of Jake and Lady's children have moved out into their own places except <a href="/wiki/T.V." title="T.V.">T.V.</a>
</li><li>The special occasion phone is the third phone that Finn used. The others were in "<a href="/wiki/Go_With_Me" title="Go With Me">Go With Me</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/Reign_of_Gunthers" title="Reign of Gunthers" class="mw-redirect">Reign of Gunthers</a>."
</li><li>The Squirrel from "<a href="/wiki/Up_a_Tree" title="Up a Tree">Up a Tree</a>" makes a cameo.
</li><li>Finn also displays his well-hidden masochistic behavior once again in this episode, just like in "<a href="/wiki/Five_Short_Graybles" title="Five Short Graybles">Five Short Graybles</a>" where he admits to liking the pain.
</li><li>This was Lady Rainicorn's first appearance since "<a href="/wiki/Jake_the_Dad" title="Jake the Dad">Jake the Dad</a>."
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Errors">Errors</span></h3>
<ul><li>At the start when Finn got BMO to try and beat-up the Jakesuit, outside the window you could see it was grey and stormy, in the next scene, when Finn jumped out of the window, it was sunny and clear.
</li><li>After Finn breaks his feet loose of Jake's arm, while being lowered into the volcano, he is shown with them bound again. 
</li><li>When you see Jake in Finn's mouth, Finn's teeth and tongue do not move while he is talking.
</li><li>In "The Silent King," the Jakesuit has a tail; however, in this episode, the Jakesuit does not.
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Production_notes">Production notes</span></h3>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Storyline_analysis">Storyline analysis</span></h3>
<ul><li>Due to the fact that Flame Princess gets angry with Finn for embarrassing her without doing any more than yell, she has probably gained control over her anger. She had a much larger reaction in "<a href="/wiki/Incendium" title="Incendium">Incendium</a>" when she caught people whispering.
</li><li>Flame Princess also made a point of inviting "less evil" members of her family, backing up her statement in "<a href="/wiki/Vault_of_Bones" title="Vault of Bones">Vault of Bones</a>" that she prefers good to evil.
</li><li>Finn is shown having an extremely high pain tolerance, especially to fire. When Finn and Jake arrive near the volcano, the Fire Wolves lap and claw at Finn, and he is unfazed by the pain. Moments later, Jake dips Finn's skullcap in molten lava. Afterwards, Finn celebrates his victory over Jake in their bet by climbing into Jake and, using the Jakesuit, voluntarily leaps into the mouth of the volcano, resulting in them both being badly burned. This may be due to his relationship with Flame Princess.
</li></ul>
