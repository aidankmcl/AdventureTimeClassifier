<p>"<b>The Witch's Garden</b>" is the fourteenth episode in the first season of <i><a href="/wiki/Adventure_Time" title="Adventure Time">Adventure Time</a></i>. It is the fourteenth episode overall.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span></h2>
<p>After Jake steals one of the <a href="/wiki/Witch" title="Witch">Witch</a>'s donuts, she steals <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>'s powers and won't give them back unless he apologizes to her. During a series of events trying to find a way to get Jake's powers back, <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>'s life is then held on the line, but will Jake's new laziness get the best of him?
</p>

</script><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span></h2>
<p>While Finn and Jake are stalking a frog holding a crown, the frog goes through a gate and Jake uses his powers to go over the wall. Soon, they stumble upon a <a href="/wiki/Witch%27s_garden_(location)" title="Witch's garden (location)" class="mw-redirect">garden</a> filled with sweets, doughnuts, cupcakes, etc. growing on bushes. Jake begins to sniff a doughnut to find out if it contains any poison. Suddenly, the owner of the garden, a witch, arrives and accuses Jake of eating one of her doughnuts. Jake disagrees on this, but sees the doughnut in his hand and eats it. Enraged, the witch casts a spell on Jake and suddenly his body from the neck down is transformed to resemble a small man wearing underwear. The witch explains that she took away Jake's powers and will not give them back unless he apologizes to her. Jake refuses to, and so the witch teleports Finn and Jake out of her garden. Then she starts talking to a <a href="/wiki/Bagel" title="Bagel">bagel</a> and—upon realizing the bagel is not a doughnut—she transforms it into a small humanoid bagel wearing underwear too.
</p><p>Finn tries to help Jake get his powers back, but now that Jake is powerless, he is lazier than ever. He remembers as a puppy, he rolled around in a mud puddle, and he comes to the conclusion that he got his powers from that same puddle. Finn decides to help him find the puddle and head out to find some mud puddles. As they start their journey, Jake becomes too lazy, making Finn carry him to the mud puddle. They arrive at <a href="/wiki/River_of_Junk" title="River of Junk">River of Junk</a>, where they see a mud puddle on the other side. Finn begins to swim through the river to reach it, but Jake's subconscious decides that it is too hard to try to reach the other side. Finn makes a catapult to get Jake over to the other mud puddle, but the mud puddle did not help Jake regain his powers back. Suddenly, <a href="/wiki/Gary_the_Mermaid_Queen" title="Gary the Mermaid Queen">Gary</a> (a mermaid) appears from the River of Junk and kidnaps Finn (after a battle), taking him to her nest and leaving him as food for her <a href="/wiki/Hatchlings" title="Hatchlings">hatchlings</a>. Finn is unable to fight back, however, because he has been knocked unconscious by the mermaid's sludge attack.
</p><p>Jake tries to rescue Finn, but is too lazy to climb up the tree and even asks for help to get up there. With no choice, Jake decides to go back to the witch and apologize to her. The witch allows Jake to get into her garden, but she refuses to accept Jake's apology, as he took too much time. Now the witch makes Jake do humiliating stunts, like slow-dancing with his subconscious with flowers in his underwear. But then, Jake's subconscious dies, as he was the subconscious of Jake's old magical self. Sad that his subconscious is dead and his best friend is going to be eaten by giant birds, Jake begins to cry about what will happen to him. The witch, planting cupcakes, murmurs, "Let this be a lesson to you, cupcakes."<i> </i>Thinking Jake learned his lesson, the witch gives Jake his powers back and restores his normal body, while his Subconscious comes back to life. Jake then knocks away the witch's cane, takes another doughnut, and when the witch asks if Jake learned his lesson, he replies simply, "Nope." Jake returns to the mermaid's nest and pushes the mermaid back to the River of Junk, then scares away the birds. When Finn asks Jake if he got his powers back by apologizing, Jake lies and says he found the right mud puddle.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span></h2>
<ul><li>The witch's spell "Magicusnomoreicus" seems to give the target a man-baby body and white briefs, as both Jake and the bagel are given them.
</li><li>This is the first episode of the show to air after the network changed their logo on May 29, 2010.
</li><li>The outside of the witch's garden looks like a monster's face with two windows for eyes, a branch nose, and a mouth made of two roots.
</li><li>The River of Junk is shown beneath the ruins of a two-storey freeway with only the pillars and a few stretches left.
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cultural_references">Cultural references</span></h3>
<ul><li>The episode is similar to the story "<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beauty_and_the_Beast" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:Beauty and the Beast">Beauty and the Beast</a>" for two reasons:
<ul><li>The way the witch overreacts when Jake plucks the doughnut from her garden is similar to how the Beast reacts when the heroine's father takes a simple rose.
</li><li>Jake is punished by being turned into something else like the Beast.
</li></ul>
</li><li>The witch's appearance is a reference to the appearance of Kayako from the 2004 horror movie <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Grudge" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:The Grudge">The Grudge</a></i>.
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Episode_connections">Episode connections</span></h3>
<ul><li>Jake steals and eats the witch's doughnut without realizing it, just as he stole some boots but did not realize it in the episode "<a href="/wiki/City_of_Thieves_(episode)" title="City of Thieves (episode)">City of Thieves</a>."
</li><li>The witch uses the magic word "Razzamafoo," which was previously used in the episode "<a href="/wiki/Wizard_(episode)" title="Wizard (episode)">Wizard</a>." The spell appears to be used for teleportation.
</li><li>The Witch saying Jake was lying but in reality is not is similar to the episode "<a href="/wiki/Another_Way" title="Another Way">Another Way</a>," when the <a href="/wiki/Forest_Cyclops" title="Forest Cyclops">Forest Cyclops</a> believes Finn is lying when he is not.
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Errors">Errors</span></h3>
<ul><li>In the credits, the name of the voice actress of <a href="/wiki/Gary_the_Mermaid_Queen" title="Gary the Mermaid Queen">Gary</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Hatchlings" title="Hatchlings">Hatchlings</a> is misspelled as "<a href="/wiki/Kerri_Kenney-Silver" title="Kerri Kenney-Silver">Kerri Kenny-Silver</a>."
</li><li>When <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> jumps out the window, he gets bruises on his face, but when he gets up, they disappear.
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Production_notes">Production notes</span></h3>
<ul><li>In a cut scene shown in one of the storyboards, the witch's video does end up on her newsletter, and a bunch of princesses and <a href="/wiki/Lady_Rainicorn" title="Lady Rainicorn">Lady Rainicorn</a> watch it on a computer.
</li></ul>
