<p>"<b>Beautopia</b>" is the fourteenth episode in the third season of <i><a href="/wiki/Adventure_Time" title="Adventure Time">Adventure Time</a></i>. It is the sixty-sixth episode overall.
</p>
<nav id="toc" class="toc"><div id="toctitle"><h2>Contents</h2><span class="toctoggle">[<a href="#" class="internal" id="togglelink" data-show="show" data-hide="hide">show</a>]</span></div><ol></ol></nav>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Susan_Strong_(character)" title="Susan Strong (character)">Susan Strong</a> returns and asks <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> to help fight off the <a href="/wiki/Lub_Glubs" title="Lub Glubs">Lub Glubs</a> and regain her people's home in <a href="/wiki/Beautopia_(location)" title="Beautopia (location)">Beautopia</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span></h2>
<p>Finn and Jake are inside one night, discussing things about coffee, when they hear banging. All of a sudden, Susan Strong bursts through the wall from the outside. She says that she needs Finn's help because of his "Hero Heart." She also adds that she needs Finn's magic, the "Red Flower," or Fire.
</p><p>Jake tries to convince Finn that Susan's completely crazy. Despite this, Finn still wants to help and tells Jake that he will help her, whether he is coming with him or not. Jake remarks that he is only coming to be "disruptive and obnoxious."
</p><p>The duo are taken to the underground cave in which the <a href="/wiki/Hyooman_tribe" title="Hyooman tribe">Hyooman tribe</a> lives. Susan thinks they should swim, and dips Finn's head in the water. Jake stops Susan shortly, saving Finn from drowning. Finn takes off his hat while catching his breath; Susan sees that Finn has no gills. She then decides they take a boat ride down the river and Jake sings "<a href="/wiki/I%27m_On_a_Boat" title="I'm On a Boat">I'm On a Boat</a>." A pool toy passes them, and Susan panics, telling both Finn and Jake to keep quiet, because it was a Lub Glub. They attempt to cross the Clashing Gates and pass, but the boat is broken. Eventually, they reach the city and several Lub Glubs come out. Jake is convinced that they are just normal pool toys and hits one with his <a href="/wiki/Stretchy_Powers" title="Stretchy Powers">Pick-Axe Hand</a>. The Lub Glubs all turn into their true form and attack the group, to Susan's terror. Susan helps Finn fight and Jake goes to put the fire in the <a href="/wiki/Beautopia_(location)#City_Heart" title="Beautopia (location)">City Heart</a>. When he does, all of the Lub Glubs die, leaving only their pool toy shells.
</p><p>It cuts to all of the Hyoomans moving back to the city. Susan tells Finn he should stay, but Finn claims he is not like her. Susan takes his hand and puts it under her hat onto her neck. Finn is surprised by what he felt and says "<i>Susan...</i>" but is interrupted by Jake, who bought a bunch of soft pretzels. As they leave, Finn waves goodbye to Susan.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span></h2>
<ul><li>The title of this episode is a portmanteau of the words "beautiful" and "<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/utopia" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:utopia">utopia</a>."
</li><li>The clashing gates are carved to look like two hands fist bumping.
</li><li>When Finn takes his hat off, his hair is seen slightly longer than it was in "<a href="/wiki/Mortal_Folly" title="Mortal Folly">Mortal Folly</a>."
</li><li>During their journeys, Susan's comments imply that she has tried to return to Beautopia in the past. (i.e. "Never made it this far before..." and "Getting close to the Lub Glub's mash-em-ups.")
</li><li>When Finn, Jake and Susan arrive at <a href="/wiki/Beautopia_(location)" title="Beautopia (location)">Beautopia</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Lub_Glubs" title="Lub Glubs">Lub Glub</a> is chewing on a skull that is wearing a hat that resembles <a href="/wiki/Fionna" title="Fionna">Fionna</a>'s.
</li><li>On the title card (inside the "I" in Beautopia), there appears to be a woman wearing a hat resembling the one worn by Finn/Fionna.
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cultural_references">Cultural references</span></h3>
<ul><li>Susan refers to fire as "red flower." In Rudyard Kipling's <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Jungle_Book" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:The Jungle Book">The Jungle Book</a></i>, fire is also called "red flower."
</li><li>Continuing the references to the television series <i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lost_(TV_series)" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:Lost (TV series)">Lost</a></i> that were in "<a href="/wiki/Susan_Strong_(episode)#Cultural_references" title="Susan Strong (episode)">Susan Strong</a>," the Lub Glubs are similar to the show's Smoke Monster.
</li><li>When Susan leaves the <a href="/wiki/Hyooman_tribe" title="Hyooman tribe">Fish People</a> she says "I'll be back," which is a quote by Arnold Schwarzenegger in the <i>Terminator</i> movies.
</li><li>Jake's clam-shaped head with a woman inside refers to the famous painting <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Birth_of_Venus_(Botticelli)" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:The Birth of Venus (Botticelli)"><i>The Birth of Venus</i></a> by Sandro Botticelli.
</li><li>The clashing gates are most likely a reference to the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Symplegades" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:Symplegades">Clashing Rocks</a> in Greek mythology.
</li><li>The Lub-Glubs in their true form resemble <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shoggoth" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:Shoggoth">Shoggoths</a>.
</li><li>When approaching the clashing gates, Finn exclaims "Balzacs" which is a reference to the 19th century French writer <span style="color:rgb(37,37,37);font-family:sans-serif;font-size:14.44444465637207px;line-height:24.88888931274414px;"><a  class="external text exitstitial" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Honor%C3%A9_de_Balzac">Honoré de Balzac</a>, here acting as a language filter for profanity. </span>
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Errors"> Errors </span></h3>
<ul><li> When Susan descends into the manhole, the hatch is still on top. However, she punched it clean off during the events of "<a href="/wiki/Susan_Strong_(episode)" title="Susan Strong (episode)">Susan Strong</a>".
</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Storyline_analysis">Storyline analysis</span></h3>
<ul><li>During the scene where a Lub Glub is chewing on what appears to be Fionna's hat, there is a large piles of skulls in the background. The skulls are almost all wearing animal-themed hats, and one skull in particular wears what looks like Finn's hat, only black instead of white. This means that the Hyoomans most likely got their hats in Beautopia, possibly leftover from a store that was selling them before the place was destroyed (the entirety of Beautopia is made up of shops and restaurants).
</li></ul>
