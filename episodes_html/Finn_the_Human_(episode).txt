<p>"<b>Finn the Human</b>" is the first episode in the fifth season of <i><a href="/wiki/Adventure_Time" title="Adventure Time">Adventure Time</a></i>. It is the one hundred&#160;and&#160;fifth episode overall.
</p>
<nav id="toc" class="toc"><div id="toctitle"><h2>Contents</h2><span class="toctoggle">[<a href="#" class="internal" id="togglelink" data-show="show" data-hide="hide">show</a>]</span></div><ol></ol></nav>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> chase <a href="/wiki/The_Lich_(character)" title="The Lich (character)">the Lich</a> through a dimensional portal where they meet <a href="/wiki/Prismo" title="Prismo">Prismo</a>, who tells them that the Lich wished for the extinction of all life. This leads Finn to wish that the Lich "never even ever existed."<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[2]</a></sup> and the creation of the <a href="/wiki/Farmworld" title="Farmworld">Farmworld</a> reality.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span></h2>
<p>After being pulled into the portal, <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> follow the Lich into <a href="/wiki/Prismo" title="Prismo">Prismo</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Time_Room" title="Time Room">Time Room</a>. They arrive just in time to see the Lich vanish after making his wish which was the extinction of all life. After a short conversation with Prismo, Finn wishes that the Lich had never existed and is promptly transported into the reality created by his wish.
</p><p>After being called in by <a href="/wiki/Finn%27s_Mom" title="Finn's Mom">his mother</a>, who is holding a baby, <a href="/wiki/Farmworld_Finn" title="Farmworld Finn">Farmworld Finn</a> is told to go into town and sell their mule <a href="/wiki/Bartram" title="Bartram">Bartram</a>. Farmworld Finn protests, but his mother insists. He follows her gaze out the window to see <a href="/wiki/Finn%27s_Dad" title="Finn's Dad">his father</a> being pushed around by members of the "<a href="/wiki/Destiny_Gang" title="Destiny Gang">Destiny Gang</a>." He is seen next talking to Bartram trying to comfort himself, when he notices the mule is missing. He wanders a little ways to find Bartram in a tree. While going to get him down, he falls into a hole covered by underbrush and <a href="/wiki/Farmworld_Jake" title="Farmworld Jake">Farmworld Jake</a> follows him. Farmworld Finn looks up to see the skeleton of <a href="/wiki/Ice_King" title="Ice King">Simon Petrikov</a> with the crown under a frozen <a href="/wiki/Mushroom_bomb" title="Mushroom bomb">bomb</a>.
</p><p>When he sees the <a href="/wiki/Ice_King%27s_crown" title="Ice King's crown">Ice King's crown</a>, he thinks to sell it instead of Bartram and goes to retrieve it. As he nears it, the decrepit <a href="/wiki/Farmworld_Marceline" title="Farmworld Marceline">Farmworld Marceline</a> jumps out from behind the bomb with a gun. She tells him to back off and that he cannot have the crown. She then tells him of how Simon died when the great mushroom bomb was deployed. He used his ice magic to freeze the bomb, but was crushed in the process. The crown, stricken with grief over the death of its master, then unleashed its power and froze the entire earth for 400 years. For 600 years, an aged and decrepit Farmworld Marceline has been guarding the crown to ensure it never harmed anyone again.Â 
</p><p>After she finishes her story, Finn merely tells her that "none of that is true" and that "magic is fake." He tells her that she lost it while living in the cave and proceeds towards the crown. Farmworld Marceline then fires the gun, but finds it is broken and does not harm Farmworld Finn. Farmworld Jake then distracts Farmworld Marceline while Finn grabs the crown. After Finn is gone, Marceline has an argument with the corpse of Simon (She tells herself the voice is not real: it is just in her head.) and she goes after the crown.
</p><p>Now in the <a href="/wiki/Junktown" title="Junktown">Junktown</a> market, Farmworld Finn tries to sell the crown. A man says he will take it, but he has no money and offers to sing at Finn's house instead. <a href="/wiki/Tromo" title="Tromo">Tromo</a>, one of the destiny gang, takes the crown and gives it to <a href="/wiki/Big_Destiny" title="Big Destiny">Big Destiny</a>, under the pretext that "property of old dead person go to Big Destiny" and refuses to give money to Finn. Finn realizes that the crown was not really his and moves on. Marceline, however, yells that the crown is hers and that she is half-demon. Destiny then tells Tromo to jack up Farmworld Marceline and <a href="/wiki/Trami" title="Trami">Trami</a> to take Bartram for dinner. Trami takes Bartram and runs up to their mansion with Destiny.
</p><p>As Tromo is attacking Marceline, Farmworld Finn shoves his <a href="/wiki/Finn%27s_flute" title="Finn's flute">flute</a> into his nose, and then <a href="/wiki/Choose_Bruce" title="Choose Bruce">Choose Bruce</a> throws lettuce at him. Tromo then retreats into the mansion saying that Finn has made a big mistake. Farmworld Finn promises Farmworld Marceline that he will return the crown to her. Choose Bruce then gives Finn a sword for his mechanical arm to which Finn responds approvingly, stating that it "feels natural, like peeing outside."
</p><p>He then runs up to the mansion and down the hall toward Bartram, but is tripped by Destiny. Farmworld Finn gets up and begins to sword fight with Destiny, but is knocked down. Finn demands his stuff back and Destiny gives it to him as his "last possession."
</p><p>Destiny then shows Finn that he had his henchmen burn the market. Farmworld Finn grabs Bartram and the crown and runs into the market place. Choose Bruce is weeping and tells him to go save his family. On his way, he grabs Farmworld Marceline and pulls her up on Bartram. When he arrives at his house, the gang has already set it on fire (Destiny is somehow already there.) with his parents and infant brother trapped upstairs. Finn asks Marceline if the crown is magic and she tells him it is very dangerous magic. He then puts the crown on his head, and the power begins to flow through him.
</p>
