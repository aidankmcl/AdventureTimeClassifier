<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcripts">Transcripts</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode starts when <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> finishes building <a href="/wiki/Flame_Princess" title="Flame Princess">Flame Princess</a>' new house, which Flame Princess loves.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Well, looks like your new house is done. [<i>Throws helmet</i>] Whoop! Jake! You wanna check out the new house?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a></b>:  Naah, [<i>Draws Lady Rainicorn on the dirt</i>] don't let me interrupt you. You two have fun.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Flame Princess</b>:  [<i>Excited</i>] I wanna see inside! [<i>Checks inside the house and merging with it</i>] [<i>Laughter</i>] I love it! [<i>deep voice</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Great! [<i>Fire touches Finn</i>] [<i>Quick sigh</i>] This doesn't hurt as bad as it used to. [<i>Fire spreads throughout Finn's right leg</i>] [<i>Then panics</i>] Oh gob! Oh gob! Oh gob! [<i>Dives into the nearby river</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Worried</i>] Finn!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn rises above the surface</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Haha, I'm okay!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Flame Princess</b>:  [<i>Interested</i>] Hmmmm...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Laughter and splashes</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Flame Princess</b>:  Oh! [<i>Requests</i>] Do the thing that I like.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Agrees and acts</i>] [<i>Sucking some water and squirts it upward like a sprinkler</i>]
