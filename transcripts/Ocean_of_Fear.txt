<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Patrick_McHale" title="Patrick McHale">Pat McHale</a></b>:  In the dark recesses of the mind, a disease known as fear feasts upon the souls who cannot overcome its power...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn and Jake chase a Fire Newt to the ocean</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a></b>:  Come back, thief!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The <a href="/wiki/Fire_Newt" title="Fire Newt">Fire Newt</a> spews fire at Finn and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a></i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Whoa!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Fire Newt</b>:  Stop farting on me! It's gross!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  You can't just go around stealing boots.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Yeah, and you know the penalty for stealing boots. It's poots... on newts.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Both</b>:  [<i>Laughs</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The Fire Newt wades into the ocean to avoid Finn and Jake</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  He's getting away in that lake!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Dude, that isn't a lake. That's an ocean.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake stretches and heads towards the ocean, barking</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Laughs and runs after Jake</i>] Bark! Bark! Bark! Bark! ...Huh?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The hue of the ocean and sky changes to red and ominous music plays as a wave crashes</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn backs away from the water as it washes onto land</i>]
</dd></dl>
