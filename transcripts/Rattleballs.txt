<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> sitting at a table in a dark room, humming to himself, and patting the table. <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a> enters.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: [<i>yawns</i>] [<i>turns on the light</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Good morning, Princess!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: [<i>screams</i>] Finn! You scared me!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: I am sorry, Princess!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: What are you doing here, Finn?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Since I got out of my last relationship, I've been slackin' on the job. So I'm rededicating my life to protecting, serving, and meeting your every need. <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> said I was acting bananas about the whole thing and wanted no part of this.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: It's 5:30 in the morning.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: That's right, Princess. I'm gettin' back on the job hardcore, giving 100% of my being all for you.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: Thank you, that's very nice. [<i>opens fridge</i>]
</dd></dl>
