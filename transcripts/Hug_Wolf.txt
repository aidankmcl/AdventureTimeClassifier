<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins showing a <a href="/wiki/Tree_of_Blight" title="Tree of Blight">black tree</a>. It turns to show its face.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a></b>:  The Tree of Blight.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The tree roars in the center of a dark forest; the camera pans out to show Finn and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Every hundred years, it spews evil spores across the land.
</dd></dl>
