<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins at the Candy Hospital. A crowd is hovering over <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a> as she's pushed on a gurney.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Nurse_Pound_Cake" title="Nurse Pound Cake">Nurse Pound Cake</a></b>:  What's her condition, <a href="/wiki/Dr._Ice_Cream" title="Dr. Ice Cream">Dr. Ice Cream</a>?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Dr. Ice Cream</b>:  She's totally gross over 90% of her body. The other 10% is crazy nasty.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Nurse Pound Cake</b>:  Will she make it, Doctor?
