<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode opens with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> standing at the edge of a large canyon.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: What'cha think?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: I can make that driz, no problem.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: It's crazy far, man.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Naw, that's just a perspectal illusion. It looks far 'cause the cliff's lower.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: Eh, go for it.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn backs up, runs, and jumps into the canyon, making it about halfway across.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Whoo! Shoot!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake <a href="/wiki/Stretchy_Powers" title="Stretchy Powers">stretches</a> arms to catch him and pulls him back up</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Almost.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: Not really.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: What? Watch me, fool! [<i>jumps again</i>] Hup. WAAAAAAH!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake jumps down after him, grabs onto Finn's back, and shapeshifts like a flying squirrel. They fly out of the canyon and continue gliding.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: I did it! Psych. [<i>smacks lips</i>] Hey, you know what? This is cool, just having bro time for once.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: What, like, no ladies?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Or dudes, or whatevs. It's just a chore sometimes.
</dd></dl>
