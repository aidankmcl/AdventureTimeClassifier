<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>In <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>'s mouth, <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> walks up to a rotten, blackened tooth.</i>]
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: [<i>groans in pain</i>]
</dd></dl>
