<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a></b>:  You want some more eggs, <a href="/wiki/BMO" title="BMO">BMO</a>?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  Yes, please.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Gives BMO more eggs</i>] How 'bout you, Finny? Need a refill?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a></b>:  I'm pretty full, man... but lemme see if I can... rearrange. [<i>Tries to move food in his stomach but fails</i>] Nope. Can't do it.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  I don't know if stomachs work like that, dude.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Sometimes it helps.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Can't argue with that! [<i>Relaxes</i>] Ahh...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Relaxing</i>] Ahh...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  [<i>Moving arms up</i>] Ahh...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Telephone rings and Jake groans.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Answering</i>] Hello? <a href="/wiki/Lady_Rainicorn" title="Lady Rainicorn">Lady</a>, it's gonna be fine. Mm-hmm. Alright, I love you, too. Bye. [<i>Hangs up</i>]
</dd></dl>
