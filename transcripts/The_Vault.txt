<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode starts off in the <a href="/wiki/Tree_Fort" title="Tree Fort">Tree Fort</a> with pots and pans crashing. <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> is stirring a bowl with nothing in it. Something growls and green tentacles come towards Finn.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Wha—?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn steps back and the stove melts, turning into <a href="/wiki/Shoko" title="Shoko">a blue creature with green hair</a>. The creature smiles.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Wha—?!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The scene goes to <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>, who is sound asleep. In the background, you can hear Finn scream for help.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: What? Finn?!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The scene goes back to Finn, where the blue creature is engulfing him.</i>]
</dd></dl>
