<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins at the <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum%27s_Castle" title="Princess Bubblegum's Castle" class="mw-redirect">Candy Castle</a>. The camera zooms in as <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a> speaks in voice over.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>:  <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>? Finn? Finn! Where are you? I need you to try this!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn is waiting outside Princess Bubblegum's room, looking inside</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I'll be there in a sec! [<i>takes phone from backpack, calls <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a></i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  What's the status?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Good, man!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Nice! Seal the deal, bro!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>laughs nervously</i>] Okay, man! Whatevs!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  You can do it, you hear me?! [<i>quickly</i>] I'm playin' <a href="/wiki/BMO" title="BMO">BMO</a>—call me later, bye! [<i>hangs up</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  How's Finn's date?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  I think it's goin' good.
</dd></dl>
