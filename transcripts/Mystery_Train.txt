<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> sleeping and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> placing a blindfold over his eyes. Jake is being held in the air by floating balloons and is wearing a birthday hat. He takes off the hat and blows into it, which creates a loud noise and awakens Finn, who jumps out of his sleeping bag.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Happy birthday!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Jake! Is that you? I can't see! [<i>Moves arms around blindly</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Right! That's because-
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I'm blind?! Jake! I'm blind on my birthday!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  No dude, you're blind<i>folded</i>.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Feels blindfold</i>] Oh...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [Puts hands on the blindfold] Keep the blindfold on. I've got a surprise for you.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The scene transitions to Jake floating down while carrying a still-blindfolded Finn in front of the <a href="/wiki/Candy_Kingdom" title="Candy Kingdom">Candy Kingdom</a>. Jake unties the balloon strings and they float off.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Takes blindfold off of Finn</i>] Surprise!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Takes the blindfold out of Jake's hand</i>] Orange blindfold? That <i>is</i> a surprise!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  That's not the surprise. This is!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Camera panels out onto a train.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  You got me a train? [<i>Hugs Jake tightly</i>] Thank you, Jake!
</dd></dl>
