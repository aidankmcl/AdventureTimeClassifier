<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins at <a href="/wiki/Iceberg_Lake" title="Iceberg Lake">Iceberg Lake</a> where <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> is lighting a flamethrower with a <a href="/wiki/Flambits" title="Flambits">flambit</a></i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Yeah! [<i>Laughs and torches an iceberg</i>] Yeah!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The iceberg melts, exposing several objects inside</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I found another bike and more computers. What do you got?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  I keep finding baby shoes!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake torches an iceberg with his flamethrower and exposes more baby shoes</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a></b>:  WHAT THE HECK, MAN?! And they're all lefties! Sorry I'm not finding any loot.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Keep it together, chubby, because I think we found enough scrap to finish building our gauntlet dock; a dock that is also a gauntlet.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Aww. Building this gauntlet dock is hard, man. Hard work sucks.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Don't be lazy, Jake.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>There's a bubbling noise coming from the lake behind Finn and Jake</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Huh?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Incoming iceberg!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Whoa! That one's huge!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  I got dibs! Ooo. I hope it's not baby shoes.
</dd></dl>
