<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The scene shows a few odd trees next to a mountain in an open shot, there are some clouds visible in the background. <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> round the mountain's corner into the shot and look at one another knowingly. Jake jumps to one tree and across to the base of the mountain, doing creative jumps and swirls with his powers all the way.</i>]
