<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins in the <a href="/wiki/Tree_Fort" title="Tree Fort">Tree Fort</a>. <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> is reading <a href="/wiki/The_Ooo_Review" title="The Ooo Review">The Ooo Review</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> is trying to play a game on <a href="/wiki/BMO" title="BMO">BMO</a>, who is playing up-tempo electronic music and dancing.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  BMO, turn the music down. BMO! Turn it down!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  [<i>Increases volume.</i>]
