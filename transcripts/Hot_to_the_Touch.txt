<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i><a href="/wiki/Flame_Princess" title="Flame Princess">Flame Princess</a> slaps <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>, leaving a hand-shaped burn mark on his face.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Flame Princess</b>:  Don't ever mess with me again!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Flame Princess shoots out the window in a trail of fire.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Looks at <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a></i>]; Who <i>was</i> that?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Still terrified</i>] The Princess of the <a href="/wiki/Fire_Kingdom" title="Fire Kingdom">Fire Kingdom</a>?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Looks out the window, then back at Jake</i>] Dude, I think I have a crush.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn walks in front of Jake.</i>]
</dd></dl>
