<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The scene begins at the <a href="/wiki/Tree_Fort" title="Tree Fort">Tree Fort</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a></b>:  And as it waded through the carnage that it had wrought, the vampire smashed their skulls just for the fun of it!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a></b>:  No way!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Yes way, it did. And also the vampire hunched over its victims and breathed their vaporized blood mist.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>warbling with tongue</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Ah, jeez! Jake, is this stuff you're saying true? Or are you just trying to mess me up? You have to be honest.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Oh, it's true, man. I heard it through a reliable source.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Reliable? Rats!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Yeah. Some say it haunted this very tree!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  This tree?!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>ominously</i>] Good niiiight...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Jake?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake warbling downstairs</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  You're full of it, Jake!
</dd></dl>
