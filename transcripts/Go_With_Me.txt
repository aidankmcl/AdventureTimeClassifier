<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> having a picnic and watching the sunset.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Hey, man, it's <a href="/wiki/Couples_Only_Movie_Night" title="Couples Only Movie Night">Couples Only Movie Night</a> tonight. Who are you bringin'?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I don't know. Who are you bringin'?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  I'm bringing my girlfriend, dude.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Oh.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>A duck walks up to them and Finn picks it up.]</i>
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I'll take this duck!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  It's couples night! You gotta bring someone you can smooch.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Man, I don't want to be smooched!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Don't have to smooch. You just have to bring someone you <i>can</i> smooch. It's, like, the rules.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Lame. [<i>Attempts to kiss duck, but it flies away.</i>] Ah! Well, I'll take this blanket! [<i>Kisses blanket.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Put the blanket down. [<i>Pats the ground beside him.</i>] Have a seat. I want to explain some "<i>things</i>" to you about couples.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Later that day, Finn and Jake are back at the treehouse. Finn is looking for someone to call.]</i>
</dd></dl>
