<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> walking through some bushes, looking for <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: [<i>calling</i>] Jake! Jake! [<i>coming out of bushes</i>] Jake? [<i>sees Jake's viola resting on a rock</i>] Hmmm... [<i>picks it up and starts playing it poorly</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: [<i>off screen</i>] Aaaah! [<i>comes out of bushes covering his ears</i>] Aaaaah! [<i>rolls on ground</i>] Bl-bl-bl-bl-bl! [<i>gets up and knocks the viola out of Finn's hands</i>] Uh! That sounded horrible, man! [<i>picks up viola</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: I don't know how to play.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: Exactly! [<i>starts tuning viola</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Distant screams are heard off screen.</i>]
</dd></dl>
