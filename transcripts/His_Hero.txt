<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Swamp_Giant" title="Swamp Giant">Swamp Giant</a> falling from a great height. They are both gripping onto the giant.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Swamp Giant</b>:  AAAAH!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Last chance, Swamp Giant! Tell us where you hid the <a href="/wiki/Mini_Queen" title="Mini Queen">Mini Queen</a>!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Swamp Giant</b>:  NEVER!!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The three scream as they fall. Squeaking is heard.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Huh? [<i>He finds the Mini Queen.</i>] Hi, Mini Queen! Jake! I've got 'er!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Then hold my hand, dude! [<i>Finn does so.</i>] Whoooop!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake inflates, making himself and Finn reach terminal velocity sooner and float gently to the ground.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Swamp Giant</b>:  Aaaah! Oof!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The swamp giant hits the ground. Finn and Jake land on the flattened giant. Jake lets his air out and deflates.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  My inflatable angel.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Heh heh heh. [<i>They jump off the giant.</i>] How's the Mini Queen?
