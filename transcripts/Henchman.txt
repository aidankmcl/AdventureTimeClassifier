<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins in an area filled with bones where Finn and Jake are playing.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Hahahaha! Here I go!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Yeah!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Sliding down skeleton</i>] Whoo-hoo!!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn hits the ground, and he, Jake, and many bones are propelled into the air. <a href="/wiki/Marceline" title="Marceline">Marceline</a> watches them discreetly.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Marceline</b>:  Hmm...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn and Jake land.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Awesome! [<i>He and Jake laugh. Suddenly, crying is heard.</i>] Someone needs our help!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn runs to the source of the crying. Jake spits out a bone and follows. They find Marceline talking to an <a href="/wiki/Old_Man_Henchman" title="Old Man Henchman">old man</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Marceline</b>:  [<i>To her henchman</i>] What is it about "henchman for life" that you don't understand?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Old Man Henchman</b>:  Oh, come on!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Whispering to Jake</i>] Dude! It's Marceline the Vampire Queen!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake screams and cowers in fear.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Old Man Henchman</b>:  Can't you find the compassion to release this poor old body?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Marceline</b>:  "Henchman for life" means, "henchman for life!!"
