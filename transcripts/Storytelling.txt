<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode starts out with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> inside the <a href="/wiki/Tree_Fort" title="Tree Fort">Tree Fort</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>moaning sickly</i>] Blankie me. Blankie me, Finn!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I'll get your blanket dude, where is it?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Right here. [<i>touching the blanket on right side of his bed</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Why don't you just get it yourself?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Because I'm sick! [<i>making a shriveled face</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Poor guy...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake is moaning sickly again. Finn puts the blanket on Jake.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Too hot! [<i>pushes the blanket off</i>] URGGH!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Oh man, Jake, is there anything I can do to make you feel better?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Soup me, Finny.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The scene cuts to another scene where Finn is holding soup for Jake.</i>]
</dd></dl>
