<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>Episode starts at <a href="/wiki/Castle_Lemongrab" title="Castle Lemongrab">Castle Lemongrab</a>. A gatekeeper is comparing <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>'s passport picture to <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>:  The other one. [<i>switches passports for the gatekeeper</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Gatekeeper</b>:  [<i>grunts</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Scene switches to Finn, who is talking to <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> on an old-fashioned telephone.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>on phone</i>] Finn? Finn?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Sorry.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Finn, I'm bored. Are you sure Princess Bubblegum doesn't like me anymore?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Yeah. That's what she said.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Sounds weird. Are you lying so you can do this dinner thing alone with Bubblegum?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  No way!
</dd></dl>
