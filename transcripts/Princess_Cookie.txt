<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with two <a href="/wiki/Candy_helicopter" title="Candy helicopter">candy helicopters</a> hovering above <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>, <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a>, who are hiding behind a <a href="/wiki/Banana_Guard" title="Banana Guard">Banana Guard</a> barricade in front of a candy store in the <a href="/wiki/Candy_Kingdom" title="Candy Kingdom">Candy Kingdom</a>. Inside the building is <a href="/wiki/Princess_Cookie_(character)" title="Princess Cookie (character)">Cookie</a>, holding several candy people hostage and walking around with a cudgel. He squints through the glass doors.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Cookie</b>: [<i>muttering</i>] Donkin' Princess...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: [<i>through a megaphone</i>] Okay, okay. How about I give you a big cowboy hat? Then will you let the hostages go?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Cookie</b>: [<i>exits store</i>] No... no! [<i>brandishing cudgel</i>] Don't play games with me, Princess. I want that crown. No crown, no hostages.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: Well, obviously that's going to be a problem, because <i>I'm</i> the princess and I need my crown. So...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Cookie</b>: No, Princess! You are problemâ€”the problem princess! [<i>beat</i>] Just gimme that crown! [<i>goes back inside</i>]
</dd></dl>
