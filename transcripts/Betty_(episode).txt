<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode opens to Bella Noche opening his eyes in a secret chamber.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Bella_Noche" title="Bella Noche">Bella Noche</a></b>: Have you obtained the staff of <a href="/wiki/Stranson_Doughblow" title="Stranson Doughblow">Stranson Doughblow</a>?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Forest_Wizard" title="Forest Wizard">Forest Wizard</a></b>: Yes, Bella Noche. We procured the staff using the full extent of our wizardy ways.
</dd></dl>
