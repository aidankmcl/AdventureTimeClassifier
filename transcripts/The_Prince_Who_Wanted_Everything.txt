<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>Ice King wakes up in a forest</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Ice King</b>: Huh? Wha-? Where am I? <a href="/wiki/Gunter" title="Gunter">Gunter</a>?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>He is found tied to a tree</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Ice King</b>: Gunter?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>He finds his legs attached to a stick by rope</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Ice King</b>: I don't think this is Gunter.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>A bonfire has been lit suddenly. <a href="/wiki/Lumpy_Space_Princess" title="Lumpy Space Princess">Lumpy Space Princess</a> appears</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Lumpy Space Princess</b>: It's me, Ice King.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Ice King</b>: Lumpy Space Princess?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Lumpy Space Princess</b>: Yeah.
</dd></dl>
