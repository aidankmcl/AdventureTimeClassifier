<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>Newborn birds are chirping in their nest as the father bird reads a newspaper with his back turned. The babies fall out. The camera pans down the tree past banners saying "HAPPY BIRTHDAY," "<a href="/wiki/Charlie" title="Charlie">CHARILE</a>," "<a href="/wiki/Viola" title="Viola">VIOLA</a>," "<a href="/wiki/Jake_Jr." title="Jake Jr.">JAKE JR.</a>," "<a href="/wiki/T.V." title="T.V.">T.V.</a>," and "<a href="/wiki/Kim_Kil_Whan" title="Kim Kil Whan">KIM KIL WHAN</a>."
