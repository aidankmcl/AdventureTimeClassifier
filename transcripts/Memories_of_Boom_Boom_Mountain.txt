<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode starts in <a href="/wiki/Marauder_Village" title="Marauder Village">Marauder Village</a> where two <a href="/wiki/Marauders" title="Marauders">Marauders</a> are refilling their mugs</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Squid_Bartender" title="Squid Bartender">Squid Bartender</a></b>:  You guys sure you want another round of squid ink? [<i>Laughs</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Two Marauders drink their mugs and then one smashes his over the other's head.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Head Marauder</b>:  [<i>The camera pans to show many marauders rough-housing in pairs. Then it shows Finn and the Head Marauder fighting.</i>] You're not too bad for a human boy!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a></b>:  Not too bad!? I'm better than that! [<i>Hears Mountain Man crying</i>] [<i>Pauses</i>]Huh? [<i>Throws the Head Marauder out of the way</i>] Jake! Do you hear that?
</dd></dl>
