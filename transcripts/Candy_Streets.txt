<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> are playing with cats who are pretending to push shopping carts in the <a href="/wiki/Candy_Kingdom" title="Candy Kingdom">Candy Kingdom</a> while <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a> looks at them.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: SLAM!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn and Jake start laughing. Scene changes to Jake's cat sitting at a desk. Finn makes his cat wipe all of the stuff of the cat's desk.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: [<i>Jake makes his cat pretend to say:</i>] Hey! I was trying to read that!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: That book is above your level.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Scene changes to Finn and Jake standing up with the cats in business suits.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: What business are you into?
</dd></dl>
