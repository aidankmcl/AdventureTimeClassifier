<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins in <a href="/wiki/Marceline%27s_cave" title="Marceline's cave">Marceline's cave</a> just outside of <a href="/wiki/Marceline" title="Marceline">Marceline</a>'s house. <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> is beat-boxing and Marceline is strumming her <a href="/wiki/Ax_bass" title="Ax bass">ax bass guitar</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Marceline</b>:  Thanks for helping me record, Finn.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  No problem, Marceline.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Marceline</b>:  Now, I'm gonna sing something really personal, so don't laugh at me.
</dd></dl>
