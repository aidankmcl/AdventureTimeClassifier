<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode starts in the <a href="/wiki/Candy_Kingdom_Hospital" title="Candy Kingdom Hospital">Candy Kingdom Hospital</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Doctor_Princess" title="Doctor Princess">Doctor Princess</a></b>: [<i>Examining <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>'s arm</i>] Hmm....
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: What is it?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Doctor Princess</b>: It's looking droopier.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: But I've been pounding pickle juice like I was preggos. For the electrolytes.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Doctor Princess</b>: Well, switch back to water. But what I really want to know, is how are you feeling Finn?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: What? Oh, I don't know. [<i>imitates trumpet tooting</i>],Â I guess I don't feel anything. Bleh. [<i>slides off the bed</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Doctor Princess</b>: Okay, that's what I thought. You gotta bounce back, man. You got burned by your bad dad, you lost your arm, and all the ladies say you don't talk to <a href="/wiki/Flame_Princess" title="Flame Princess">Flame Princess</a> no more.
</dd></dl>
