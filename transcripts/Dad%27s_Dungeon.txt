<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  What? Your head on my body isn't boring! It's <i>weird</i>! [<i>Giggles</i>]

<dl><dd>[<i>The group notices sparkles on the Tree Fort's leafroof.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Sparkles on the house? <i>LET'S SQUISH 'EM!</i>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  No. They're around this holo-message player. It's got a cartridge with it.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Oh, snap! [<i>Claps once</i>] Well, plop that cartridge in the slot, playah!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Yeah! Okay! [<i>Inserts cartridge into holo-player</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The holo-player activates, and the late <a href="/wiki/Joshua" title="Joshua">Joshua</a> walks into view in the 3D holo video.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Joshua [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  [<i>Clears throat</i>] Hello, boys. [<i>Coughs</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn and Jake [<i>In unison</i>]</b>:  <i>Dad!!</i>
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Joshua [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  If you're hearing this prerecorded hologram message, it's because I passed on, and my <a href="/wiki/Spirit_sparkles" title="Spirit sparkles">spirit sparkles</a> guided you to its secret hiding place. Right now, I'm holding both of you in my hands. You're both still little squishy babies. [<i>Finn cries and Jake chuckles. Joshua scowls at Finn who is still crying. He lets them both crawl away. Joshua has a disappointed look on his face.</i>] I made you boys something. It's a <a href="/wiki/Joshua%27s_dungeon" title="Joshua's dungeon">dungeon</a>. A proper dungeon. Full of evil monsters, traps, and magic. The whole kazoo!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn and Jake</b>:  Whoa! Kickin'! Kickin'!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Joshua [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  Now, this next part of the message is just for you, Jake, so Finn, cover your ears. [<i>Finn covers his hat's ears.</i>] Jake... really, this dungeon is for Finn. I know I won't be around forever, and I wanna make something that will force Finn to toughen up.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  What?!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Joshua [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  Now, tell Finn to uncover his ears now.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Dude, take your hands off your head.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  <b>WHAT?!?!</b>
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake slaps Finn's arms off his head.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Joshua [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  Alright, boys. Now to give you some incentive, at the end of the dungeon, I'm going to put the <a href="/wiki/Demon_Sword" title="Demon Sword" class="mw-redirect">Family Sword</a>. It's made out of Demon's Blood.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Just then, the <a href="/wiki/Demons#Blood_Demon" title="Demons">Blood Demon</a> appears behind Joshua in a wall of flames.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Whoa, what the--?!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Whoa, dang!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Blood Demon [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  Give me back my blood, Joshua!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Joshua [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  <a href="/wiki/Kee_Oth_Rama_Pancake" title="Kee Oth Rama Pancake">Kee Oth Rama Pancake</a>.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Blood Demon [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  Waaaaah!! [<i>Banished by Joshua's spell, he disappears in flames</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Whoa!!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Geez-louise!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn and Jake high-five.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Joshua [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  The dungeon's eighty paces west of here under a <a href="/wiki/Dumb_Rock" title="Dumb Rock">dumb-lookin' rock</a>. And Finn, this dungeon's gonna kick your tail. I bet you won't even get past the first trial, ya whiny baby!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The player deactivates.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  What?? What was <i>that</i> about?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Uh... [<i>Makes an "I don't know" noise and shrugs</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Scene switches to outside in the grasslands. Finn and Jake are near the dumb-looking rock.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Dumb Rock</b>:  Duh... duh... du-ugh... duh...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn pushes the rock aside revealing a hole. He jumps down.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Whoo! [<i>He reaches the floor and sees a pile of burgers and a pile of hotdogs. Jake drops down.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Whoa! Burgers and hotdogs! Yeah, yeah, YEAH! [<i>Runs towards the food</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Wait, Jake!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Stops</i>] But... burgers and hotdogs..

<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I don't think those burgers and hotdogs are burgers and hotdogs at all.

BOO!] and the <a href="/wiki/Hamburger_Monster" title="Hamburger Monster">Hamburger Monster</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Hot_Dog_Monster" title="Hot Dog Monster">Hot Dog Monster</a> awaken. <dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Aaaahh...

<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Slaps Jake</i>] DUDE!! [<i>Notices the next cartridge on a pedestal</i>] Look!

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Man... again? [<i>Does it</i>]
</dd></dl>

<dl><dd><b>Joshua [<i>On tape</i>]</b>:  Butts are for pooping! Do it for Poppy!

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Over here! [<i>Jake runs towards him.</i>] Check it out, Jake. I found two ways. This way has some kinda <a href="/wiki/Flower_Path" title="Flower Path">flower trap</a>, and that way... has <i>that</i> guy.
