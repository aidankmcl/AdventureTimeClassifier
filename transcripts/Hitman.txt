<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>Episode starts in the <a href="/wiki/Breakfast_Kingdom" title="Breakfast Kingdom">Breakfast Kingdom</a>. <a href="/wiki/Breakfast_Princess" title="Breakfast Princess">Breakfast Princess</a> is eating breakfast alongside <a href="/wiki/Toast_Princess" title="Toast Princess">Toast Princess</a> in a bed. <a href="/wiki/Ice_King" title="Ice King">Ice King</a> peers at them through a window. He starts laughing.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Ice King</b>:  Good morning, Breakfast Princesses. [<i>He sneakily climbs in through the window. He comes up beside their bed with a jar of honey and a honey dipper. He drips honey on Breakfast Princess's waffles.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Breakfast Princess</b>:  Huh? [<i>Ice King looks up at them, mumbling incoherently and then suddenly frowns.</i>] Sis, give me the phone. [<i>Toast Princess hands her a phone from beside their bed. Breakfast Princess dials a number.</i>] [<i>talking on phone:</i>] Oh, hey, <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>. I got another Ice King situation here. Oh, yeah? That would be great! Thanks.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> barge through a door in the room.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Ice King?! How many times have we warned you about using love potion?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Ice King</b>:  That's why I'm using honey.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Man, who cares if it's honey? You stink anyways.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>laughs</i>] Yeah, you stink so much I'm gonna ground you for a week!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Ice King</b>:  What? You can't do that!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  You're grounded for two weeks!
