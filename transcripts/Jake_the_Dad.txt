<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode starts out with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/BMO" title="BMO">BMO</a> walking up to <a href="/wiki/Lady_Rainicorn" title="Lady Rainicorn">Lady</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Lady_Rainicorn%27s_house" title="Lady Rainicorn's house">house</a></i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  Puppies! Puppies! Puppies!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>They enter the house</i>] Hey <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>! Hey Lady! We came to see your new pups!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  [<i>BMO jumps out through Finn's legs and the door</i>] Puppies!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Lady</b>:  얘들아 안녕! ("Hi guys!")
</dd></dl>
