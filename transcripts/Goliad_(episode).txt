<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> trying to form a cube with his body while <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> is trying to build a stick fort using twigs</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  What'ya building?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn stops building the stick fort.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Um, it's just a little stick fort.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Oh, rad!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake shrinks to a suitable size to play with the Stick Fort</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Look. It's just my size.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn and Jake both laugh while Jake climbs onto the top of the fort</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Role-playing</i>] Hey, get away from my fort, you big stinky monster!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I like it when you get small, Jake.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake picks up a leaf from one of the sticks</i>]
