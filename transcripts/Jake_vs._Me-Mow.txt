Wildberry Princess kneels down to put her oven mitt in a drawer and when she rises she notices a small hole in a pie but simply shrugs and takes the pies to a table where <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> are sitting.</i>]

[<i>The cat hisses and bares her claws at Jake</i>] Whoa!
