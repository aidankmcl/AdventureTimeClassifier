<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Library" title="Library">Library</a> carrying <a href="/wiki/Rainicornicopia" title="Rainicornicopia">an enormous book</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn &amp; Jake</b>:  [<i>grunting</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  It's slippin', dude.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>They groan as they set it down on a table.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Phew. [<i>reading title</i>] Raini-corni-copia? An Unabridged History of the Rainicorns. [<i>yawns</i>] Whatever, man. I barely even made it through the title.
