<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> running through a field while eating out of a bowl of ice cream with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> in pursuit.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Jake. Jake! [<i>Catches up to him</i>] Hey man, shouldn't we conserve our energy? The <a href="/wiki/Ice_Cream_Marathon" title="Ice Cream Marathon">Ice Cream Marathon</a> starts tomorrow.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: [<i>With mouth full</i>] I know what the Ice Cream Marathon is. [<i>Swallows ice cream</i>] I've been training for weeks. And I am determined to eat more ice cream at the Ice Cream Marathon than anyone else!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: You sure like ice cream.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: Ice cream is good!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Strange croaking noise is heard off screen</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>: What the croak was that?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: It came from over there [<i>points to his left</i>].
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The two walk over to the source of the sound.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Whoa, Jake! Check it out!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>A old frog dressed in armor holding a staff is seen standing next to a small chest.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Frog</b>: [<i>Croaks strangely</i>]
</dd></dl>
