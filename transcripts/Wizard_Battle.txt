<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode starts in the <a href="/wiki/Wizard_Battle_(event)" title="Wizard Battle (event)">Wizard Battle</a> stadium, <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> is walking through almost empty stands, singing "<a href="/wiki/Wizard_Battle_(song)" title="Wizard Battle (song)">Wizard Battle</a>"</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake is asleep on a seat; Finn crawls towards him</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Whispering</i>] Wizard Battle is starting soon!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Pushes Finn away, groans</i>] I know! We got here six hours early, you dork! Just wake me up when they start selling hot dogs. [<i>sees that he is laying on a hot dog</i>] Whoa! What do you know! [<i>Eee gasp</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  The wizards are arriving!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The crowd cheers as the wizard walk in and kneel</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Whoo-hoo! Yeah!!!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Grand_Master_Wizard" title="Grand Master Wizard">Grand Master Wizard</a></b>:  [<i>Offscreen</i>] Ladies and gentlemen...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Gasps repeatedly</i>]
