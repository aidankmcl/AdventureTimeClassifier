<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with a <a href="/wiki/Frog_Carrying_Crown" title="Frog Carrying Crown">frog carrying a crown</a> walking with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> following behind.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Dude... how long are we gonna follow this frog?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I just wanna see 'im put on that crown.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The frog walks through the bars of a locked gate.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn &amp; Jake</b>:  [<i>Running towards the gate</i>] Wooow!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  It looks cool in <a href="/wiki/Witch%27s_garden_(location)" title="Witch's garden (location)" class="mw-redirect">there</a>!
</dd></dl>
