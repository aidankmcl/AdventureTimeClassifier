<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins in <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a>'s room where she is sleeping in <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum%27s_rock_t-shirt" title="Princess Bubblegum's rock t-shirt" class="mw-redirect">her favorite t-shirt</a>. The cuckoo clock above her bed sounds an alarm, dangling a cat from a spring in Princess Bubblegum's face. She wakes up.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>:  Okay, okay, I'm up. [<i>pushes cat away, gets out of bed, and sniffs her shirt deeply</i>] Ah! [<i>smacks lips</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>She opens her closet, which contains a photograph of her with <a href="/wiki/Marceline" title="Marceline">Marceline</a> on the inside of the door, and takes out a jacket which she puts on over her t-shirt.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Scene cuts to a different room in <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum%27s_castle" title="Princess Bubblegum's castle">The Candy Castle</a>, where Princess Bubblegum is speaking with her back to a curtained window.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>:  Hey, everybody. I've asked you here today because you are the best of the best.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cinnamon_Bun" title="Cinnamon Bun">Cinnamon Bun</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peppermint_Butler" title="Peppermint Butler">Peppermint Butler</a>, <a href="/wiki/Manfried" title="Manfried">Manfried</a>, and three <a href="/wiki/Banana_Guard" title="Banana Guard">Banana Guards</a> are shown facing Princess Bubblegum.
