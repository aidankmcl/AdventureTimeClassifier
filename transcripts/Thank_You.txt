<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with an <a href="/wiki/Birds" title="Birds">Alarm Clock Bird</a> entering the <a href="/wiki/Snow_Golem" title="Snow Golem">Snow Golem</a>'s house and waking him.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Snow Golem crushes the bird with his hand, but it escapes unharmed.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Alarm Clock Bird</b>:  [<i>Coughs</i>] [<i>Flies out the window</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Snow Golem</b>:  [<i>Yawns</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Snow Golem gets out of bed and walks to the bathroom. There he washes his head, then looks in the mirror and fixes his hair so that it makes snow horns, but decides not to use them.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Snow Golem gets some breakfast. He takes a box of <a href="/wiki/Flakies" title="Flakies">Flakies</a> and a bottle of milk, which actually contain a pear and acorns, respectively. He pours them into a bowl and eats.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>After eating, Snow Golem looks in his cupboard and finds that there is no more food. He draws an acorn and a pear on a piece of paper as a shopping list. He prepares to leave, putting on a small pouch and a garland of branches. He leaves his home, grabs a cart, and starts walking.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Snow Golem</b>:  [<i>hums while walking through the forest</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Snow Golem stops and sees <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> fighting the <a href="/wiki/Ice_King" title="Ice King">Ice King</a>, who is wearing <a href="/wiki/Ice_armor" title="Ice armor">Ice armor</a></i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Ice King, this the last time you steal our sandwiches!
</dd></dl>
