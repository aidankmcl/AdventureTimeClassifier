<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> in his bed sleeping and snoring loudly. A wad of paper hits him on the head. He opens his eyes in surprise, looks around, and turns to see <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> holding a piece of paper and smiling.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<p><br />
