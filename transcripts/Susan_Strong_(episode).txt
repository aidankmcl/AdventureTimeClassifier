<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcripts">Transcripts</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a> standing in a field of tree stumps.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: And here at this site, my Great Uncle <a href="/wiki/Gumbald" title="Gumbald">Gumbald</a> cut down all these taffy trees in a single night.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: 'Cause those trees were evil... right, Princess Bubblegum?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: Nah. Hey, but I bet they have a ton of stories about your <a href="/wiki/Humans" title="Humans">human</a> relatives.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>: Um... not really. I've never even met any other humans. If I think about it too much, I get all soul-searchy and weeeeiiirrrd...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>: [<i>Embarrassed</i>] Ohhh, boy... I've made things awkward. Well, let's forget about that. I brought you guys here because I need you to rip out all these stumps!
</dd></dl>
