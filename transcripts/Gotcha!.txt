<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode starts with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>, and <a href="/wiki/BMO" title="BMO">BMO</a> playing a game of bocce. BMO throws a ball.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  Jake, if I beat you, you have to call me "sensei" for a month.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Deal.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Turtle Princess goes by on a slow-moving rocket.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Turtle_Princess" title="Turtle Princess">Turtle Princess</a></b>:  Hi, guys!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn, Jake, &amp; BMO</b>:  'Sup, Turtle Princess?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>She leaves them. Scene cuts to Lumpy Space Princess swatting bugs off of herself.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Lumpy_Space_Princess" title="Lumpy Space Princess">Lumpy Space Princess</a></b>:  Agh! Agh! Agh! Agh! Agh! Agh! Get your hands <i>off</i> me, Steven!! YAH! [<i>Throws him down</i>] And you, too, Chad! [<i>Flicks ant away</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Turtle Princess enters.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Turtle Princess</b>:  Heeeey, giiiirl!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Lumpy Space Princess</b>:  Heeeey, giiiirl!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Turtle Princess</b>:  Brought you some little fruit pies.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Lumpy Space Princess</b>:  Awesome. [<i>Starts eating</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Turtle Princess</b>:  How ya doin' out here?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Lumpy Space Princess</b>:  Turtle Princess, the men out here will <i>not</i> leave me alone!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Turtle Princess</b>:  Huh... it must be nice.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Lumpy Space Princess</b>:  Nah. It's horrible. They just want me for my lumps! [<i>Vulture screeches at them.</i>] These lumps aren't for SALE, BILLY!
