<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>A thunderstorm rages outside the <a href="/wiki/Tree_Fort" title="Tree Fort">Tree Fort</a>. Inside, <a href="/wiki/BMO" title="BMO">BMO</a> is practicing karate.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>: Kya! Kya! [<i>reading karate magazine</i>] Hmm.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The magazine reads, "How to stop a man's heart with karate: 1. 'Focus chi' and 2. 'Stop a man's heart'" with diagrams depicting each action.</i>]
</dd></dl>
