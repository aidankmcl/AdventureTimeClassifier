<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn puts on crown and glows with power</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a></b>: [<i>Coughs and pushes Marceline off of Bartram with his robotic arm</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Marceline" title="Marceline">Marceline</a></b>:  I warned you, dingle.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I can hear the crown's promise of power. Invading my brain! [<i>Coughs up snow slug and ascends</i>] [<i>Uses his ice powers to put out the fire and turns toward the <a href="/wiki/Destiny_Gang" title="Destiny Gang">Destiny Gang</a></i>]. Destiny Gang! [<i>Freezes them</i>] Yay! [<i>hurls jagged shaped ice at the rest</i>]
