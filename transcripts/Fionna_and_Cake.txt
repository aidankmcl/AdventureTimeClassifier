<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode starts out in Prince Gumball's castle</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Fionna" title="Fionna">Fionna</a></b>:  Throw it, <a href="/wiki/Cake" title="Cake">Cake</a>!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Cake grabs a <a href="/wiki/Jelly_Kinder" title="Jelly Kinder">Jelly Kinder</a> and twirls it up to Fionna with her arms</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Fionna</b>:  Eyahh! [<i>Kicks it to the ceiling where others are</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Fionna</b>:  These Jelly Kinders aren't... alive, are they?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Prince_Gumball" title="Prince Gumball">Prince Gumball</a></b>:  [<i>Holding one</i>] What? No! They can't even talk.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The Jelly Kinder frantically lets out a sound and Prince Gumball hastily covers its mouth</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Prince Gumball</b>:  Kick it! Thanks for helping me out guys.
</dd></dl>
