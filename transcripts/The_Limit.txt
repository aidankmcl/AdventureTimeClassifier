<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The scene opens to the <a href="/wiki/Hot_Dog_Kingdom" title="Hot Dog Kingdom">Hot Dog Kingdom</a>, where <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> stare at the stars while two <a href="/wiki/Hot_Dog_Knights" title="Hot Dog Knights">Hot Dog Knights</a> play in the mud besides <a href="/wiki/Hot_Dog_Princess" title="Hot Dog Princess">Hot Dog Princess</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>pointing</i>] Dude! Shooting star! Make a wish!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>A small glowing orb hovers slowly towards Finn and Jake.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>chuckles</i>] That's not a shooting star. [<i>Finn grabs the orb from the air.</i>] It's just a firefly.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn kisses the firefly, leaving a glowing stain around his mouth, and releases it.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  What are you gonna wish for dude?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Oh, you know. That something special only two bros can share.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>excited</i>] I think I'm wishing for the same thing!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  You mean the...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn and Jake</b>:  <a href="/wiki/Ancient_Psychic_Tandem_War_Elephant" title="Ancient Psychic Tandem War Elephant">Ancient Psychic Tandem War Elephant</a>!
</dd></dl>
