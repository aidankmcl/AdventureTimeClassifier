<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins at the <a href="/wiki/Tree_Fort" title="Tree Fort">Tree Fort</a>. The camera pans to the bridge linking the living room and attic.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a></b>:  [<i>Laughs</i>] Ahhh... YEAH!!! [<i>Busts through living room door and lands on bridge.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a></b>:  [<i>Entering</i>] Heh heh. [<i>Deep voice</i>] Aw, yeah. [<i>Chuckles</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Jake, get ready for a slap in the jowls. [<i>Slaps Jake's nose; Jake does a snorting laugh</i>] Seriously, though... get ready... for a day filled with adventure!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Whoa, really?!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Yeah, man!! [<i>He slaps Jake's nose again, making him snort and laugh again</i>] I wrote on my arm this to-do list of cool junk. [<i>The clouds begin to get darker and bigger</i>] Number one: back flip off this bridge and do the splits. Number two: track down and slay a goblin horde. Number three...[<i>Glances up at thundering clouds</i>] storm clouds?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Examining</i>] Hmmm... [<i>Knives fall from the clouds and Jake gasps.</i>] Finn... it's a knife storm!! [<i>Finn slaps Jake's nose yet again</i>] It's raining knives, man! Come on! [<i>Exits</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  I've never seen a <a href="/wiki/Knife_storm" title="Knife storm">knife storm</a> before... It's beautiful.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Knives begin falling near Finn; Jake pulls Finn inside before knives can fall on him.</i>]
</dd></dl>
