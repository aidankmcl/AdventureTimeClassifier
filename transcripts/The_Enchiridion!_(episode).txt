<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>The episode begins with all of the <a href="/wiki/Candy_People" title="Candy People">Candy People</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>, dancing in the <a href="/wiki/Candy_Kingdom" title="Candy Kingdom">Candy Kingdom</a></i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Candy Party! Jake! This party is <i>so</i> crazy!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  I know, Finn! I know! [<i>Laughing</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b><a href="/wiki/Gumdrop_Lass_1" title="Gumdrop Lass 1">Gumdrop Lass 1</a></b>:  So, Finn... Do you have a girlfriend?
</dd></dl>
