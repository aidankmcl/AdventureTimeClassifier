<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>Episode starts at <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Tree_Fort" title="Tree Fort">Tree Fort</a>. <a href="/wiki/BMO" title="BMO">BMO</a> climbs into a dark room with assorted objects.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  Hello?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>BMO climbs down a ladder to get into the room. He finds a cardboard box and hides underneath it. He starts chuckling. On his screen, a digital hand selects some files. The hand drags the files into an onscreen trash can, deleting them. BMO's screen glitches, and BMO seems woozy. He chuckles and then looks normal. Scene switches to outside of the box. We hear BMO doing the same act once more and then BMO gets out of the box. He still seems dizzy. We see him selecting the file "CORE SYSTEM DRIVES.sys" and then deleting it. The scene shows his face, which turns from a smile into a frown. BMO's screen glitches out wildly as BMO moans. BMO gets up and wanders around for a second, before falling over. The screen is blank.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  BMO?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Scene shows Finn and Jake looking down at something. The image is distorted.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  BMO?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>BMO is lying in a bed. BMO wakes up.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  BMO, are you okay?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  What did you do?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>BMO</b>:  It wasn't me. I didn't do noth- do noth- do nothing. [<i>BMO repeats 'do noth-' because of his system glitching.</i>]
</dd></dl>
