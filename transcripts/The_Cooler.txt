<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>In the Fire Kingdom, a Flame Person is sleeping on his bed. His alarm clock rings. The Flame Person wakes up and shuts it off by putting his hand on it.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Flame Person</b>: [<i>shivers</i>] Brrrr. It's freezing in here. [<i>teleports into his huge pants</i>] Whoa, I'm losing a lot of weight! Calorie control and modern exercise are the key. But I don't do any of those things, so this is weird. Hmm...
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The Fire Kingdom is shown to be cooled down. The Flame People were discussing about their changes.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Flame Guard</b>:  My jets are tiny! [<i>covers his jets</i>] No one look at my tiny jets!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Flame Person</b>: At least your body is normal, man. My body isn't hot anymore!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Flame Person</b>: What's going on, man? I'm scared!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Flame Person</b>: Give me a hug, man! I don't know! [<i>hugs the Flame Person. The Flame Person then vanishes in his arms.</i>] Aaaaaah! Nooooo! My baby brooooo!
</dd></dl>
