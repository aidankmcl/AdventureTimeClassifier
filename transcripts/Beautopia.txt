<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>It is night and raining outside of the Tree Fort; Finn is sitting at the table while Jake puts on some hot water.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Hey, what kind of coffee do you want?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Hazelnut! Hazelnut! What if your name was "Zelnut"? And then I would be all like "Hey, Zelnut."
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  That's terrible.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Hey, Zelnut.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Irritated, Jake grows and gently bodyslams Finn, who laughs.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Nooooo! Stop!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>There is then a knocking sound.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  You hear that?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Yeah.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>The wall is then burst through by none other than <a href="/wiki/Susan_Strong_(character)" title="Susan Strong (character)">Susan Strong</a>. She stands still as lightning flashes</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn and Jake [<i>In unision</i>]</b>:  Susan Strong!?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Susan</b>:  Finn, help Susan.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Of course I will.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Excuse us for a moment, Strong. [<i>Jake takes Finn aside.</i>] Dude, you know you're my bro, but that girl is bad news.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  What? Naw.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  She's crazy, man. She's a <i>fish person</i>!
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

<dl><dd><b>Susan</b>:  [<i>Examining Finn's head</i>] You no gills. [<i>Finn puts his hat back on.</i>] We take boat.

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Oooh... I'm on a boat with a couple of wackos. Shakin' my hips, and dippin' my fat toe... In the water! Dippin' in the water! This party's gettin' hotter! It's so hot... it's stupid. So where we headed, Susan?
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Oh, no. Oh, no. No, no, no! No, Susan, no!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>They go down the whirlpool, and Finn and Jake scream; they pop out safely out of a pipe.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Laughs</i>] That was awesome. See, Jake? We can trust Susan. She's on the trolley.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake just morphs his head into a fish person's, points to Susan, and makes a "screwball" sign with his arm.</i>]
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  [<i>Laughs</i>] Quit acting like a clam, you ham.
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Hey, you hearing that too?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Susan</b>:  Getting close to the Lub Glubs' mash-'em-ups.

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Balzacs! 
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Susan</b>:  The clashing gates!
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Susan</b>:  [<i>Rubbing Jake's head</i>] 'S okay. Susan know when is safe to pass through.
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Susan!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Susan</b>:  [<i>Tosses the lantern</i>] Finn! Jake!
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Susan</b>:  Red flower! Throw it to city heart!
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Finn and Susan bump fists, and Susan grabs his arms.</i>]
</dd></dl>

<dl><dd>[<i>Susan swings him at a Lub Glub.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Susan</b>:  [<i>Looking at Finn</i>] Hmm.

</dd></dl>

Okay, well, here I go. [<i>Swinging the lantern</i>] one, two, three... [<i>The giant Lub Glub grabs his arm and roars] [In a trance</i>] That reminds me of... my mother. [<i>Jake struggles against the Lub Glub and tosses the lantern into the furnace.</i>]
</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>

</dd></dl>
