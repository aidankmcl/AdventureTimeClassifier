<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transcript">Transcript</span></h2>
<dl><dd>[<i>Episode starts in the <a href="/wiki/Candy_Kingdom" title="Candy Kingdom">Candy Kingdom</a>. <a href="/wiki/Finn" title="Finn">Finn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jake" title="Jake">Jake</a> are laughing while throwing bottles at a nearby wall.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  Here, try throwing this one.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake hands Finn a bottle. Finn throws the bottle. When it breaks, a purple vapor in a humanoid form appears and vanishes.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  [<i>Jake picks up an orange bottle.</i>] Here's one called Caturday Surprise.
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  Throw it!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd>[<i>Jake throws the bottle. Green, pink, and blue cats explode out of it. Both Finn and Jake start laughing. They stop their laughter once they hear <a href="/wiki/Princess_Bubblegum" title="Princess Bubblegum">Princess Bubblegum</a>.</i>]
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Princess Bubblegum</b>:  Yeah, go on, get out of here! [<i><a href="/wiki/Duke_of_Nuts" title="Duke of Nuts">An unknown figure</a> runs away from Princess Bubblegum who is standing at the entrance of the Candy Kingdom.</i>] Unless you feel like a fist cookie, you better keep runnin'!
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Finn</b>:  What the heck is goin' on?
</dd></dl>
<dl><dd><b>Jake</b>:  I have no idea. But it's probably best to stay out of it.
